About Me

Hello! My name is Kamil Piziak. I am currently a Senior at Hunter College, pursuing a Bachelors in Computer Science, with a minor in Mathematics. Born in Brooklyn, NY, I have always been a passionate learner about everything! However, one thing I have always been passionate about is computers. Growing up, I have always loved video games, and the process of making them. I started to learn more about making video games, and I was introduced to the field of Computer Science! I became an avid lover of coding, and since then I have strived to learn more about Computer Science!
